Balinea Holiday Home was lovingly renovated in May 2024 by its German-Indonesian owners. Authentic Alang Alang roofs, lush tropical greenery, and vibrant Balinese colours create an atmosphere that is both exotic and deeply welcoming.

At its heart lies a stunning 16-metre shared pool — a centrepiece of calm set within beautifully landscaped tropical gardens.
